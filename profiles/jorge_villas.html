Jorge Villas

Student at Instituto Nacional

Jorge Villas called for a vote from about 30 of his classmates to decide whether to stage a toma, or student takeover, at their high school.

Students from Instituto Nacional, one of Chile’s most prestigious high schools, had met at the University of Chile administration building where their college counterparts were camping out.

“We have a basic right as students to a free quality education,” Villas told his classmates. “Even though it’s a long and hard process, we have to do something because the adults in our country aren’t doing anything.”

A toma at Instituto Nacional began a few days later.
