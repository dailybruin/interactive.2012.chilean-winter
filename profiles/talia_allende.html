Talia Allende

Creating Awareness

Talia Allende took a break with her friend at an Aug. 28 protest, the most heavily attended student movement event of the year and the pinnacle of what organizers had dubbed “Student August.”

That day, an estimated 150,000 students marched down some of Santiago’s busiest streets.

“I like to protest because people take pictures of me, and that creates awareness,” said Allende, an art student at the University of Chile. “I think our message is clear: What we want is education.”
