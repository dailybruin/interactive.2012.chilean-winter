Miguel Ramirez

Protesting until the end

High school student Miguel Ramirez paraded through empty streets following a student protest on Aug. 28.

As leftover tear gas trickled down side streets, alleyways morphed into foggy landscapes littered with burning piles of torn banners.

“[The gas] is horrible,” Ramirez said. “You can’t breathe; your eyes hurt. It’s worth it though, because our system is totally unfair.”
